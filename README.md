## About Nyx Dynamics

Nyx Dynamics consults at the intersection of **clinical expertise**, **AI validation**, and **health equity**. Core services include:

- AI Readiness Assessment
- Algorithm Validation
- Academic Research Support
- Community Research Development
- Clinical AI Training
- Strategic Advisory

> *"An algorithm that appears to have all the answers is dangerous. One that names its limitations invites trust."*
> — The Readiness Paradox

- Hosted on **Netlify** with custom domain via **Namecheap**
- Particle systems and glow effects via vanilla CSS/JS
